Below is the full **13-moon tiered naming system** in **three parallel registers**:

* **Original** = the version I gave you before
* **Serious** = more corporate, productizable, less overtly mythic
* **Mythological** = more symbolic, antique, literary, or cosmological

For each moon I keep the same four layers:

* **Initiative**
* **Team / codename**
* **Product**
* **Internal verb**

Then I explain why each register works.

---

## 1) Wolf Moon

### Original

* **Initiative:** Wolf Moon
* **Team / codename:** Lupa
* **Product:** Chorus
* **Internal verb:** coordinate

### Serious

* **Initiative:** Wolf
* **Team / codename:** Signal
* **Product:** Mesh
* **Internal verb:** coordinate

### Mythological

* **Initiative:** Moon of the Pack
* **Team / codename:** Fenrir
* **Product:** Long Cry
* **Internal verb:** bind

**Why these choices**
The core pressure here is **group survival through signaling**.
The **Original** version is the best balance: **Lupa** has just enough mythic weight, and **Chorus** turns pack-voice into a credible product metaphor.
The **Serious** version strips the animal imagery down into network logic: **Signal** and **Mesh** are cleaner if this were real infra.
The **Mythological** version pushes fully into saga territory: **Fenrir** and **Long Cry** feel older, more fated, and less commercial.

# My judgment on the strongest versions

If the goal is **actual product naming**, the best overall picks are usually:

* **Wolf:** Original
* **Snow:** Original or Serious
* **Worm:** Original
* **Pink:** Original
* **Flower:** Original
* **Strawberry:** Original
* **Buck:** Original or Serious
* **Sturgeon:** Original
* **Harvest:** Original
* **Hunter:** Original
* **Beaver:** Original
* **Cold:** Original or Serious
* **Blue:** Serious

That is interesting because the **Original** register mostly wins. It hits the balance point between usability and symbolic charge. The **Serious** register is better only when the original gets too stylized, and the clearest case of that is **Blue Moon**, where **Sideband** is stronger than **Outofband**.

The **Mythological** register is best when the naming system is for:

* internal initiatives,
* fiction,
* houses/factions,
* a game,
* or a literary project rather than a startup.

I can also do one more pass where I produce a **hybrid best-of list**: one final chosen version for each moon, selecting from Original, Serious, and Mythological to make the strongest single canon.
